## â• Registering for a New Role

### Method 1: Using the Banner (Easiest)

If you see a **green banner** at the top of the page saying:
> "Unlock More Features! Register as Provider or Labour"

1. Click the **"Register Now"** button
2. You'll be taken to the Role Management page
3. Continue with the steps below

### Method 2: Through Settings

#### Step 1: Go to Settings
1. Click your **profile picture** in the top right
2. Click **"Settings"** from the dropdown menu
3. Or go directly to: `/settings`

#### Step 2: Navigate to Manage Roles
1. Look for **"Manage Roles"** in the settings menu
2. Or go directly to: `/settings/roles`

#### Step 3: View Available Roles
You'll see cards for all roles:
- âœ… **Enabled roles** - Roles you currently have
- ğŸ†• **Available roles** - Roles you can register for (with "Available" badge)

#### Step 4: Enable a New Role
1. Find the role you want (Provider or Labour)
2. **Toggle the switch ON** (it will turn green)
3. You'll see a **"New!"** badge appear
4. If the role requires setup, you'll see a yellow info box with details

#### Step 5: Save Changes
1. Click the **"Save Changes"** button at the bottom
2. Wait for the success message: "Roles updated successfully!"

#### Step 6: Complete Onboarding (Automatic)
After saving, you'll be **automatically redirected** to set up your new role:

**For Provider:**
- You'll see a welcome screen with steps to add equipment
- You can follow the guide or click "I'll Do This Later"
- Go to `/provider/equipment` to add your first equipment

**For Labour:**
- You'll see a profile setup form
- Fill in:
  - âœ… **Skills** - Select from common agricultural skills
  - âœ… **Experience** - Years of experience
  - âœ… **Daily Rate** - Your rate per day (required)
  - â­• **Hourly Rate** - Your rate per hour (optional)
  - â­• **Bio** - Tell farmers about yourself
- Click **"Complete Setup"** to save your profile
- Or click "I'll Do This Later" to skip

#### Step 7: Start Using Your New Role
- The **role switcher** will now appear in the header (if you have 2+ roles)
- You can switch between your roles anytime
- Each role has its own dashboard and features

## â“ FAQ

**Q: Can I have multiple roles at once?**  
A: Yes! You can be a Renter, Provider, AND Labour all at the same time.

**Q: How do I know which role I'm currently using?**  
A: Look at the role switcher button in the header. It shows your active role.

**Q: Can I disable a role after enabling it?**  
A: Yes, go back to `/settings/roles` and toggle it OFF. But you must keep at least one role active.

**Q: Do I have to complete the onboarding?**  
A: No, you can click "I'll Do This Later" and complete it whenever you want.

**Q: Where do I add equipment after becoming a Provider?**  
A: Go to `/provider/equipment` or use the dashboard navigation.

**Q: How do farmers find me after I register as Labour?**  
A: Your profile appears in the labour listings at `/labour` once you complete your profile.

**Q: Can I change my labour rates later?**  
A: Yes, you can update your profile anytime from your dashboard.
