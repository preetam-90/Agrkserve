**Target Audience:** Indian farmers, equipment owners, agricultural laborers

## üìê Logo Composition

### **Layout Options (Create 3 Variations)**

#### **Option 1: Icon + Wordmark (Horizontal)**

- **Left Side:** Square/Rounded square icon container
- **Right Side:** "AgriServe" text
- **Proportions:** Icon takes 30-40% of width, text takes 60-70%

#### **Option 2: Icon Above Wordmark (Vertical)**

- **Top:** Centered icon in rounded container
- **Bottom:** "AgriServe" text centered below
- **Best for:** App icons, social media profiles

#### **Option 3: Minimal Icon Only**

- **Just the tractor/agricultural icon** in the branded container
- **Best for:** Favicon, small applications

## üîß Icon Design Specifications

### **Central Icon: Modern Tractor**

Create a **stylized, minimalist tractor icon** with these features:

#### **Tractor Style:**

- **Modern silhouette** (not too detailed, not too abstract)
- **Side profile view** facing right
- **Large rear wheels, smaller front wheels** (recognizable tractor proportions)
- **Cabin/driver compartment** visible
- **Exhaust pipe** extending upward

#### **Color Treatment:**

- **Main body:** Amber/Gold (#f59e0b) or white
- **Wheels:** Darker shade for contrast
- **Details:** Minimal lines for windows, wheels, grill

#### **Container:**

- **Shape:** Rounded square or squircle (12px border radius feel)
- **Background:** Dark charcoal (#1a1a1a) to black gradient
- **Border:** 2-3px solid amber/gold (#f59e0b) line
- **Effect:** Subtle inner glow or gradient overlay

---

## ‚úèÔ∏è Typography Requirements

### **Font Style:**

- **Type:** Modern sans-serif (Inter, Poppins, or similar)
- **Weight:** Bold/Semi-bold (600-700)
- **Case:** Capital A, rest lowercase "griServe"

### **Text Treatment:**

- **"Agri"** - Dark charcoal color (#1a1a1a or #111827)
- **"Serve"** - Emerald green color (#10b981) - this is the accent/highlight
- **Spacing:** Slight letter-spacing (0.5-1px) for modern feel

### **Accent Element:**

- **Underline bar** below "Serve" or full word
- **Color:** Emerald green (#10b981)
- **Shape:** 3-4px thick, rounded ends, width matches "Serve" text

## üìê Technical Specifications

### **File Requirements:**

- **Format:** PNG (transparent background)
- **Dimensions:**
  - Primary: 1024x1024px (square, for all uses)
  - Horizontal: 2048x1024px (for website header)
  - Icon only: 512x512px
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI minimum
- **Background:** Transparent (alpha channel)

### **Scalability:**

- Must be legible at 32x32px (favicon size)
- Must look crisp at 1024x1024px (app icon size)
- No tiny details that get lost when scaled down

## üö´ What to Avoid

1. **Overly complex illustrations** - Keep it simple and scalable
2. **Photorealistic tractor** - Should be stylized/iconic
3. **Too many colors** - Stick to the palette
4. **Trendy effects** - No heavy gradients, shadows, or 3D (flat design is better)
5. **Generic stock icons** - Must feel custom and branded
6. **Text that's too thin** - Bold, readable at small sizes
7. **Clich√© agricultural imagery** - No wheat ears alone, no old-fashioned barns
